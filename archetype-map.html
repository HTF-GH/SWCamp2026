The Archetype Map

Every mature masculine archetype has a full expression and two shadow poles — one active, one passive. Boy psychology is the immature form; Man psychology is the integrated form. This is the entire structure of Moore & Gillette’s model on a single page.

How to read each card
Full archetype (mature, integrated)
Active shadow (too much)
Passive shadow (too little)
▲ The Active Shadow
When the archetype is inflated and unregulated. The energy possesses the man rather than serving him. Too much — grandiosity, cruelty, mania.
▼ The Passive Shadow
When the archetype is denied and starved. The man is cut off from the energy entirely. Too little — collapse, weakness, emptiness.
◎ Tier I · Boy Psychology

The four immature archetypes of the boy. These are not bad — they are the seeds. Each one is meant to mature into its adult counterpart below. When a grown man is still run by these, it signals arrested development.

☉
The Divine Child
Seed of the King

The wellspring of life, joy, and possibility. The newborn sense that one matters, that the world is full of wonder. Mature form becomes the King’s calm centredness.

Shadow Poles
▲ Active
The High Chair Tyrant
Grandiose, demanding to be the centre of all things. “The world serves me.”
▼ Passive
The Weakling Prince
Listless, entitled but helpless. No vitality, no initiative, expects rescue.
⚔
The Hero
Seed of the Warrior

The most advanced boy archetype — the adolescent energy that takes on the world, proves itself, and breaks from the mother. Its job is to eventually “die” so the Warrior can be born.

Shadow Poles
▲ Active
The Grandstander Bully
All show, reckless, needs to dominate and impress. Overestimates himself.
▼ Passive
The Coward
Runs from every confrontation. Backs down, avoids the necessary fight.
✦
The Precocious Child
Seed of the Magician

The curious knower — eager to learn, to understand, to figure things out. The wonder-filled questioner. Matures into the Magician’s deep wisdom and mastery.

Shadow Poles
▲ Active
The Know-It-All Trickster
Manipulates with cleverness, deceives, deflates others to feel superior.
▼ Passive
The Dummy
Plays stupid, hides intelligence, dull and unresponsive to protect itself.
♡
The Oedipal Child
Seed of the Lover

Warmth, connection, and a deep yearning for union and meaning. Reaches out for love and the spiritual. Matures into the Lover’s passion and empathy.

Shadow Poles
▲ Active
The Mama’s Boy
Addicted to connection, dreamy, can never get enough warmth or attention.
▼ Passive
The Dreamer
Isolated, withdrawn into fantasy, disconnected from real feeling and bonds.
↓
Through genuine initiation, each boy energy matures into its adult form
◍ Tier II · Man Psychology

The four mature archetypes — the “double quaternio” of the integrated male Self. Each is a board of directors within. A whole man can access all four with balance, accessing the full energy without being possessed by either shadow.

♔
The King
Order · Blessing · Generativity

The central archetype. Brings order from chaos, blesses and affirms others, and creates a fertile realm where life can flourish. He is calm, centred, and uses power in service of the whole. Islamic parallel: khalifa — the steward who rules justly under a higher authority; the sakina of a settled heart.

Shadow Poles
▲ Active
The Tyrant
Exploits and degrades others, fears all rivals, destroys what he cannot control.
▼ Passive
The Weakling
Abdicates, lacks centredness, paranoid and unable to hold the throne.
⚔
The Warrior
Discipline · Courage · Devotion

Clarity of purpose, aggressive energy in service of something beyond himself. Disciplined, decisive, loyal to a transpersonal cause. Knows his own death and so lives fully. Islamic parallel: the greater jihad — the war against the lower self; sabr, the patient endurance of the committed.

Shadow Poles
▲ Active
The Sadist
Cruel, destructive, cuts down others, hates weakness in himself and all.
▼ Passive
The Masochist
Takes endless abuse, powerless, no boundaries, turns aggression inward.
✦
The Magician
Knowledge · Insight · Containment

The knower of hidden things, master of technology and inner truth. He sees clearly, holds secret knowledge, and counsels the King. The reflective, initiating awareness. Islamic parallel: the ‘alim and the shaykh — the one who guides through knowledge and discernment.

Shadow Poles
▲ Active
The Detached Manipulator
Withholds knowledge, manipulates from the shadows, uses truth to control.
▼ Passive
The Denying “Innocent”
Pretends not to know, refuses responsibility for his gifts, envious and stuck.
♡
The Lover
Passion · Connection · Aliveness

The archetype of feeling, beauty, and connection to all things. He is deeply attuned, sensuous, empathic, alive to the world. The energy that makes the other three worth having. Islamic parallel: mahabbah — divine love of the Sufi path; the qalb, the feeling heart that perceives the Real.

Shadow Poles
▲ Active
The Addicted Lover
Lost in pleasure and sensation, no boundaries, driven by endless craving.
▼ Passive
The Impotent Lover
Flat, disconnected, unable to feel, joyless and cut off from life’s vitality.
♔ The Whole Man
♔ ⚔ ✦ ♡

Maturity is not choosing one archetype — it is accessing all four in balance, while staying out of the eight shadows. The King gives order, the Warrior gives action, the Magician gives wisdom, the Lover gives life. Held together with the discipline of inner work, they form the integrated masculine Self. This is the territory SW Camp invites you to walk.
